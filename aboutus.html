Our Story

From a small New Jersey startup to a globally recognized web development studio

Who We Are

Phillcode is a bold, creative collective of web developers and designers with one mission — to turn raw ideas into functional, beautiful, and business-ready websites. Founded in 2022 in Kenya, we've grown from a solo developer to a team of 12 specialists serving clients worldwide.

What makes us different is our unique blend of veteran expertise (our lead developers have 10+ years experience) and fresh talent that brings innovative perspectives to every project.

We're not just coders - we're digital strategists, user experience advocates, and creative problem solvers who happen to be exceptionally good at building websites.
