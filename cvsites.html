The Evolution of CVs

From paper to PDF to interactive web experiences - how professional profiles have transformed

1980s-1990s

Paper Resumes

Typewritten or handwritten documents mailed or handed to employers. Limited to one or two pages with basic information.

  • No interactivity
  • Difficult to update
  • Single format
Late 1990s-2010s

PDF & Digital Resumes

Electronic documents that could be emailed or uploaded to job boards. More formatting options but still static.

  • Easier to distribute
  • Better formatting
  • Still limited interactivity
2010s-Present

LinkedIn Profiles

Online professional networks allowed for more dynamic profiles with recommendations and connections.

  • Network effects
  • More visibility
  • Limited customization
The Future
