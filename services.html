Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical website project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. A basic brochure website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while more complex ecommerce sites or web applications can take 8-12 weeks or longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline after our initial consultation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

Yes! We regularly work with clients across the United States and internationally. All communication can be handled via video calls, email, and project management tools.

What's included in your maintenance plans?

Our maintenance plans include security updates, regular backups, performance optimization, content updates (within agreed limits), and technical support. Plans can be customized to your specific needs.

Can you help with domain and hosting setup?

Absolutely. We can recommend hosting solutions based on your needs, purchase domains on your behalf, and handle all technical setup. We prefer working with reliable hosts like SiteGround, WP Engine, or AWS.

What if I need changes after the project is complete?

We offer several options: one-time updates billed hourly, retainer agreements for regular updates, or we can train your team to use the content management system. All our projects come with some post-launch support included.
